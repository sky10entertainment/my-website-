About Us

Sky 10 Music School is dedicated to nurturing musical talent in a fun, professional, and creative environment. Located in Ruiru, Kenya, we specialize in piano, guitar, violin, and vocal lessons for all ages and skill levels. Whether you're a beginner or an aspiring professional, our tutors are here to help you grow.
